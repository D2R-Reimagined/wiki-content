Cube Recipes

Horadric Cube recipes that players may find useful on their journeys.

Additional recipes can be found on the Item Crafting and Item Upgrades pages.

Socket Recipes

Applies to Weapons, Shields, Chest Armor and Helms only.

Jewelry, Belts, Gloves and Boots cannot have sockets.

Number of sockets is dependent on Item Level - check online sources before attempting to punch sockets.
 

*Based on item's level and max number of sockets

Unsocket Recipes

Pliers can be bought from Blacksmithing vendors in all acts.
 

*Does not work on runewords

Conversion Recipes

* Tome is returned full
** Item must have durability and cannot already be Indestructible
*** Item Affixes are rerolled

Recycle Recipes

*Tome is returned full

Reroll Recipes

100% plvl = item's level is based 100% on character's level

100% ilvl = item's level is based 100% on input item's level

* Generates the same unique item but with re-rolled stats. Corruptions and Upgrades are scrubbed clean.
** Using this recipe on a unique that is up-tiered will change the unique to one that naturally roles on that base type
