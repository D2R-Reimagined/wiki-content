Venom Whirlwind Assassin Build Guide

An interactive guide for the WhirlwindsinA hybrid Assassin build using Chaos Runeword for Whirlwind and Venom for poison damage. in Diablo II: Reimagined. Updated for April 2025.

Overview

The Whirlwind Assassin uses the Chaos RunewordFal + Ohm + Um in claws, granting +1 Whirlwind. to access the Barbarian’s Whirlwind skill, combining it with Venom for poison damage and Death Sentry for AoE via Corpse Explosion. It’s gear-intensive but excels in high-density areas like Chaos Sanctuary.

  • Pros: Unique melee playstyle, versatile damage, decent AoE clear.
  • Cons: Gear-dependent, moderate survivability, weak vs. bosses.

Skills

Maximize skills in this order for optimal performance.

  • Claw Mastery (25): Boosts claw damage and Attack Rating.
  • Venom (25): Adds poison damage to Whirlwind (0.4s duration).
  • Fade (20): Grants damage reduction and resists.
  • Death Sentry (10): AoE via Corpse Explosion.
  • Fire Blast (1–18): Synergy for Death Sentry.
  • Weapon Block (1–5): Blocks attacks with claws.
  • Cloak of Shadows (1): Blinds enemies.
  • Mind Blast (1): Stuns and converts.

Playstyle

Combine Whirlwind with traps for efficient clears.

  1. Pre-Buff: Cast Battle Orders, Fade, Venom.
  2. Engage: Lay 3–5 Death Sentry traps, use Cloak of Shadows.
  3. Whirlwind: Spin in short bursts (tri-ww) to avoid damage.
  4. Mobility: Teleport (Enigma) or Burst of Speed.
  • Chaos Sanctuary: High density, great for AoE.
  • Pits: Elite packs, good drops.
  • Cows: Fast clears with traps.

Leveling

Start as a Trapsin, respec at ~75 for Chaos.

Use Fire Blast and Wake of Fire. Stats: Vitality focus.

Transition to Lightning Sentry + Death Sentry. Farm Nightmare TZ.

Farm nightmare TZ for Chaos runes. Respec to Whirlwindsin. focus poison skill dmg % upgrades and gear.
