Super Uniques & Uniques

Super uniques are named bosses that always appear in a certain map and/or location, such as A1 Stony Field's Rakanishu or Act 5 Frigid Highlands Rectifier. They have gold names and a set property or two in addition to random properties.

Uniques are randomly generated leaders of a pack of minions that spawn in an area as you explore it. They have gold names and random properties.

 

Champions

A group of three monsters that spawn in an area as you explore it that have blue names.
