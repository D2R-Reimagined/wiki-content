General

  • Splash charm has been reworked to be a small nova centered on your target for more reliable AOE
  • Curse durations now cap at 20s (impacts both player and enemy curses)

User Interface

  • Controller Stash background reworked to be consistent with inventory and look less out of place and cluttered compared to other controller UI elements.

Classes

Melee Updates

  • Some physical damage skills now have a small amount of added flat damage/level to make +skills a bit more useful and smooth out the leveling process. Exact values will likely still need further adjustments
    • Skills include: Jab, Impale, Fend, Sacrifice, Zeal, Charge, Bash, Double Swing, Double Throw, Frenzy, Tectonic Slam, Concentrate, Berserk, Whirlwind, Feral Rage, Maul, Fury

Amazon

  • Impale has been reworked and now thrusts beyond your target, dealing damage in a line
    • No longer causes extra durability loss
    • No longer always hits
    • No longer uninterruptible
    • Max slow% reduced from 75% to 30%
    • ED% reduced from 300% and 25%/level to 70% and 15%/level
    • Attack speed massively increased (no longer uses the old attack sequence animation)
  • Added synergies for Jab, Impale, and Fend
  • Jab's %ED increased from -15% and 3%/level to 0% and 8%/level

Assassin

  • Charge duration increased to 30s from 15s for QoL

Barbarian

  • Tectonic Slam has been slightly reworked:
    • Spikes now deal 100% of source damage, up from 75%
    • Spikes are no longer effected by Slow Missiles
    • Number of spikes increased from 3 to 8 to make the area of effect more reliable
    • Now usable with polearms
  • Battle Cry tooltip updated to reflect that it reduces enemies' Physical Damage Reduction

Druid

  • Fire Claws reworked into an AOE on-hit Fire-based attack:
    • Doesn't work with splash charm because AOE is built into the skill
    • Damage curve adjusted to fit the skill's new role.
    • Skill level 29+ has a substantial damage bonus
  • Ravens mana cost adjusted from flat 6, to scale at higher levels:
    • level 1, 3.2 mana
    • level 10, 5.5 mana
    • level 25, 9 mana
    • level 41, 13 mana
    • level 76, 21.7 mana
  • Decreased Spirit of Barbs HP/lvl
  • Increased HP/lvl for Heart of the Wolverine, Oak Sage, and all Vines for Hell only
  • Fixed Carrion Vine aura giving an extraordinary amount of mana regen. It now provides an aura of 5% mana regen +5/lvl
  • Fixed carrion vine aura not giving hpregen. It now provides 5 replenish + 5/lvl

Necromancer

  • Revive monster velocity decreased from +200% to +130%
  • Fire Golem aura adjusted to use Golem Mastery as a synergy instead of +aura skill level to prevent the skill from being capped earlier than intended
  • Golem tooltips updated to show the damage bonus from Golem Mastery
  • Raise Skeleton now properly shows the %dmg increase from Raise Skeletal Mage

Paladin

  • Vengeance reworked to apply scaling tri elemental damage based on skill level and synergies.
    • Now works with splash charm
    • No longer reliant on weapon damage for elemental damage
    • Skill lvl 29+ has a high damage curve

Monsters

  • Hell Grotesque family changes:
    • %PDR reduced from 66% to 55%
    • HP reduced slightly
    • Spawnling's HP reduced slightly
  • Hell Regurgitator family changes:
    • Max HP reduced by 17% to make their HP more consistent
  • Doom Knight's max melee damage reduced by 20% to reduce variance
  • Abyss Knight and Oblivion Knight's melee damage reduced by 15%
  • Arsenal and Shenk LTs no longer always spawn with Extra Strong
  • Smith/Hephasto Swords, Tormented Sisters, and Shenk LT's can no longer spawn with auras
