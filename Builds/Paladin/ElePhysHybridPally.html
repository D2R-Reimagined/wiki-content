Overview

This build is a high-damage variant of the old “auradin” or more recent “tesladin”. With the new and improved runewords and corrupted gear, it easily handles all content in the game both solo and 8 player. — including Clone and Ubers

Key Features

Solo clearing speed: Insane clear easily wipes entire screen by just walking around.

8-player viable: Incredibly Strong with right gear set up.

Torch boss killer: Clears torch bosses with zero effort.

Minimal Mechanics: Great for players who prefer little to no effort builds and just wanna left or right click their way to fun.

Gameplay Showcase

1-Player Zone

Mobs are destroyed instantly off screen for ultimate enjoyment.

8-Player Zone

Incredibly strong in party play brings so much damage and utility to the party with auras.

Gear Setup

Weapon

Hand of Justice (Phase blade)

Grants Level 16 Holy Fire aura when equipped.

-20% to Enemy Fire Resist.

Corruption: +% All Elemental Damage
-% All Enemy Resistances.

Upgrade: 511 all weapon elemental damage, 20% Crushing Blow, 40% Deadly Strike.  You can opt for more CB here and drop the weapon flat ele damage

Helm

Dream Runeword (Bone Visage or Whatever Aesthetic you Prefer)

+15 to Holy Shock, %Max Life increase and all Res and FHR.

Corruption: Any.

Upgrade: Anything I took plus 3 skills for AR on Zeal and damage and defenses on holy shield.

Shield

Dream Runeword (High Res Pally Shield base 25-45 RES)

+15 to Holy Shock, %Max Life increase and all Res and FHR.

Corruption: literally Any for Utility, Max Res, All Res for easy resist capping.

Upgrade: +Max Res All, +All Res (x5)

Armor

Flame (Runeword)

Grants Level 17 Holy Fire aura.

+Fire Skill Damage, -20% Enemy Fire Resist.

Corruption: literally Any for Utility, Max Res, All Res for easy resist capping.

Upgrade: +Max Res All, +All Res (x5)

Boots

Stairway To Heaven (Unique Mirrored Boots)

+Fire Skill Damage, -15% Enemy Fire Resist.

Corruption:  We want Faster Cast Rate and Increased Attack Speed (you can opt for Cannot be Frozen and Drop Raven Frost for a crafted ring you'd Prefer).

Upgrade: Any for utility Life on Kill (for sustain) Dex/Vit (for Block/AR/Life) etc.

Gloves

Dracul's Grasp (Unique Vampirebone Gloves) Uber Boss Farming ((we swap to))  Lachdanan's Bracers (Unique Vambraces) for General Content

We take these for Uber boss farming for the Lifetap, open wounds life steal and Str, Swap for Lachdanans All other content or LOH 

Corruption: Ideally we want CB/DS for Ubers or Ele Dmg/FCR.

Upgrade: up to you

Belt

Megaladon Wrap (Unique Sharkskin Belt)  or Crafted Belt

+All Skills, +5–10% Elemental Skill Damage.

Prioritize 10% Elemental Skill Damage. on crafted belt

Upgrade: +All Stats, +All Res (x3)

Corruption: Increased Attack Speed/ Faster Cast Rate or Max All Res/All Res.

Accessories

Jackal's Laughter

+1 to All Skills

+6 to Terror

Adds 7-8% Mana stolen per hit

Adds 8-11% Life stolen per hit

+0.38% Deadly Strike (Per Character Level)

 +55-80 to Life

Upgrades: any

Corruption:  FCR/IAS or anything We only need enough Ias to hit the 103 Zeal/Fanat BP for max frames

Raven Frost (oldie but goodie can be replaced with crafted ring)  **if you replace it you'll need to source your CBF from corruption somewhere.

Dex, Massive AR, Cannot Be Frozen, Cold absorb Enough said

Upgrades:  any

Corruption:  we wabt FCR/IAS or anything remember we only need enough IAS to hit the 103 Zeal/Fanat BP for max frames

 

Charms & Sockets

Charms: We are going to use a Torch and Annihilus preferably Upgraded and and fully Upgraded Collins, Lightning//Phys//Fire Sunders Upgraded then focus on Large Charms with -enemy fire and light resist and Crushing Blow until we comfortably hit around 50% CB.

Sockets: Who needs em? lol.

Mercenary Setup

Act 2 Might Merc recommended

Weapon: Infinity

This is all the really matters.  We need this conviction for maximum Elemental devastation.  The rest of his kit you can select for utility.  If you need resists Humility or Sacred Aura armors are great.   Kit him out however you'd like, just keep in mind him being alive is 80% of your damage

 

 

Stats

Strength: Enough to equip gear.

Dexterity: Enough for 75% block with Holy Shield active. (If you don't want to Smite ubers and would rather Zeal invest more here until you're 85% Chance to hit)

Vitality: Dump all remaining points here.

Energy: None.

Skills

Resist Fire: Max Its a Holy Fire Synergy

Resist Lightning: Max Its a Holy Shock Synergy

Fanatacism: Required for MAX IAS freames for zeal/smite

Other Auras: Pre Requisites for Fantacism, 1 Point into Salvation.

Combat: 20 point Zeal, 5 into Holy Shield. 

((you can chose to go 1 point Zeal and put 20 into Salvation for more ELE damage but you'll not have the phys damage for very high light/fire immunes even with Conviction))

Breakpoints

IAS: Minimum 23%. Easily obtained just from our phaseblade base and the 30% ias from runeword

FCR: 75% is nice for easy teleporting but we very rarely teleport.

 

Thoughts

This pally with sub optimal gear easily clears all of P8 content and is fun to boot.  

If you're looking for some screen clearing face smashing fun give this a shot you might just find your new main.
