General

  • Dim Vision / Hit Blinds Target Curse can now stack with other Curse effects
  • Socket Orb NoDrop Chance has been increased slightly to compensate for their more wide-spread availability from monsters
  • (P1 Only) All Bosses (Normal and Terrorized) have had their base NoDrop chance decreased to 5 (was previously 15-20)
    • Players should see more drops overall as a result of this change
  • (P1 Only) All Rune Bosses (Non-Terrorized) have had their base NoDrop chance increased to 10 (was previously 5)
    • Players should see less items and more runes from Rune Bosses overall as a result of this change
  • (P1 Only) All Rune Boss Rune Tables now have a base NoDrop chance of 2 (up from 0)
    • Players should see some variance in rune drops going forward
  • Update default game icons to be in line with Reimagined Icon art.

Systems

Introducing the Item Dye system!

  • Players can dye their Weapons, Armor, Helm and Shield a variety of colors
  • Purchase dyes from gambling vendors in all acts
  • Transmute an item with a dye to change its color (even from one color to another)
  • Use the Dye Remover to return an item to its base color again
