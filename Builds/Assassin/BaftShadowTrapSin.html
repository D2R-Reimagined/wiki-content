The Chosen Wolf and his Seven Sisters

Baft's Shadow Master TrapSin Build

Introduction:

Shadow Master has a few hidden functions that are pretty neat,
The ability to use any spell in the Assassin's arsenal scaling with points invested
Summoned with ilvl 99 gear for every slot once  you're at 17 hard points 
 Combine this with a tonne of auras along with enchants from your chosen wolf merc
Then watch your band of cutthroats clear the screen whilst a valkyrie looks over them.
