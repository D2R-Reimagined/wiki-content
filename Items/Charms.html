Charms have been reworked entirely in Reimagined so that Small, Large and Grand Charms all fill a niche and provide a variety of beneficial effects.

In addition to Magic Charms, a number of new Unique Charms have been introduced. At later stages players can begin crafting their own Grand Charms to further customise their playstyle.

Furthermore, Melee Characters can obtain a special, upgradeable Small Charm that allows them to deal splash damage!

Charm Weight System

Charms have a ‘weight’ attributed to them which restricts the number you can carry in your inventory. 

If you reach charm weight 41 or more then your maximum mana is set to 0. 

You can check weight in the advanced stats panel. This applies to all Charms.

  • Small Charms have a weight of 1
  • Large charms have a weight of 2
  • Grand Charms have a weight of 3

Splash Charm - Collin's Might

The Splash Charm (Collin's Might) is an upgradeable Small Charm obtained by killing Corpsefire in the Den of Evil on Normal difficulty.

This charm is designed for melee builds. It lowers a Character's minimum and maximum damage in exchange for granting each attack an area of effect (AoE). As the charm is upgraded the amount of AoE damage increases, and the Min/Max Damage penalty decreases.

Uber Charms

The Uber Charms, Hellfire Torch & Annihilus, have both seen changes in Reimagined. Both now have upgraded versions that provide more power and utility that players can work towards.

Players are only allowed to carry one Torch and Soulstone charm at a time - trying to do otherwise will see your hero's health set to 1!


Acquiring a Hellfire Torch

Each Uber Prime Evil drops a Soul (Mephisto's Animus, Baal's Calamity, Diablo's Ire). Transmuting these three items in the Horadric Cube will produce a base level Hellfire Torch.

*Only one Hellfire Torch can be created per game.

 

 

Upgrading the Hellfire Torch

Players can incrementally upgrade their Hellfire Torch by defeating the Uber Prime Evils additional times. 

To upgrade the torch transmute it with the Standard of Heroes and an Orb of Corruption.

 


Obsidian Beacon

The Obsidian Beacon is the final form of the Hellfire Torch.

 

Acquiring Annihilus

To obtain the Annihilus charm you must sell a Standard of Heroes or a Stone of Jordan ring to a vendor.

The next Super Unique monster you encounter in the same game will be transformed into the Diablo Clone.

Killing the Diablo Clone rewards the player with a small charm called Annihilus.

 

Upgrading Annihilus

The Annihilus charm can be upgraded to a superior version called the Black Soulstone.

Combine the Annihilus and one each of the following orbs: Assemblage, Conversion, Corruption, Infusion, Shadows.

 

Black Soulstone

The Black Soulstone is the final form of Annihilus

 

Crafted Charms

You can craft various forms of Grand Charms to help push your builds to their limit.

All crafts produce guaranteed stats, and an additional 3-5 random stats from the Grand Charm Prefix/Suffix table.
