Formulas 公式

Damage Formula 伤害计算公式

Dmg = [ DmgB × ( 1 + EDW ) + DmgE ] × ( 1 + EDN + Dex ÷ 100 ) + EleDmg

DmgB = Base Damage. The damage value of weapon base item. 武器基础伤害（白字）

DmgE = Equipment Damage. Min/Max Damage value from any equipment attributes. 所有装备属性上的最大最小伤害值（蓝字）

EDW = ED from Weapon. Including that from jewelries embedded in weapon. 武器ED值，包含镶嵌在武器上的珠宝

EDN = ED from Non-Weapon. Including that from skills. 非武器ED值总和，包含技能加成

Dex = Total Dexterity points. 总敏捷点数

EleDmg = Element Damage. 元素伤害

Attack Speed Formula 攻速计算公式

AnimDuration = {(AnimLength * 256) / [AnimSpeed * (AnimRate + SIAS + EIAS - WSM) / 100]} -1
[EIAS = 120 * IASItem / (120 + IASItem)]

It's a little complex. Please see attack speed formula details.

We can lookup IAS breakpoints in D2planner or a simple calculator.

公式的具体解释参看这里。查询攻速断点可以用D2planner或者简易计算器。

HP Formula 血量计算公式

HP = [ HPB + HPCL × ( Lvl - 1 ) + Vit × HPV + HPE ] × ( 1 + HP% ) + HPEL × Lvl 

Lvl = Character Level. 角色等级

Vit = Total Vitality Points, including that from equipment. 活力点数，包含装备属性

HPB = Character Base HP. (AMA=50, Mizan=74) 角色基础HP（1级时血量）（亚马逊为50，米山为74）

HPCL = Character Increased HP per Level. (AMA=2, Mizan=10) 角色每级增加的HP（亚马逊为2，米山为10）

HPV = Increased HP per Vitality point. (AMA=3, Mizan=0) 每1点活力增加的HP（亚马逊为3，米山为0）

HPE = Fixed HP from Equipment, not HP per Level. 装备上增加的确定的HP值，非每级增加HP

HPEL = HP per Level from Equipment. 装备上每级增加的HP

HP% = Increase Max HP percentage from Equipment, including skill bonus(like BO). 装备上的增加血量上限，包含技能加成（比如BO）

Critical Striking and Piercing Formula 暴击和穿刺计算公式

P = 1 - ( 1 - PP ) × ( 1 - PS )

P = Final Probability of Double Damage or Piercing. 暴击或穿刺的最终几率

PP = Probability of Deadly Strike or Piercing Attack on Stat Panel. 属性面板上的致死打击或穿刺几率

PS = Probability of Critical Strike or Pierce from skills. 技能面板上的致命攻击或穿刺几率

Piercing Attack Hits Formula 穿刺攻击命中次数公式

For Guided Arrow:

E(H) = 5 - (1 - P) × (P4 + 2 × P3 + 3 × P2 + 4 × P)

E(H) = Avarage hit count for a single guided arrow. 单支导引箭命中怪物次数的期望值

P = Probability of Piercing. 穿刺几率

For other skills:

E(H) = 1 ÷ ( 1 - P )

E(H) = Avarage hit count if plenty of monsters. 当有足够多怪物时，理论上单支箭能穿透击中怪物个数的期望值

P = Probability of Piercing. 穿刺几率

Explanations 解释

[1] Why not Str or Vit points? 为什么不加力量和活力？

We will have enough Strength points from the crafted GC and belt, so we do not need extra Str.

We will have about 2K life after BO, so we do not need extra Vit.

超大符和腰带已经提供了足够的力量穿装备，所以不需要再加力量点。

使用BO以后可以达到2K血量，基本上死不了，所以不需要再加活力点。

[2] Why Guided Arrow but not Strafe? 为什么用导引箭而不用炮轰？

With D2RR, one shot of guided arrow can hit one single target 5 times at most just like 1.09.

It's much faster killing a boss using guided arrow than strafe with high PA(>95%).

现在可以像1.09版本一样，一发导引箭可以击中单个目标最多5次。

穿刺几率很高(>95%)，打BOSS用导引箭比炮轰要快得多。

[3] Why +25 Pierce? 为什么要加满穿刺？

Pierce is the best way for high PA.

High PA is the key point for kill bosses with Guided Arrow.

And in some scenarios with plenty of monsters, such as cow level, Multiple Shot also gets benefits from high PA.

Average damage of Guided Arrow with 25-level Pierce is about 22% higher that with 1-level Pierce.

获得高穿透几率最好的方法就是加穿刺被动技能。

高穿透是用导引箭快速击杀BOSS的关键点。

而且在有足够多怪物的场景，像牛关，多重箭也能从高穿透获得很高的收益。

穿刺加满时比只加1点穿刺，导引箭的平均伤害能高22%。

[4] Why Quicksilver? 为什么选择Quicksilver作为武器？

It is the best weapon, bar none.

Grand Matron Bow has the highest base damage than any other bows.

Although it is the slowest bow, we can easily archieve 7F attack speed, which is the highest speed, just like other bows.

With Level 9 Fanaticism Aura, we just need to get 105%IAS.

这是最好的武器，没有之一。

大院长弓的基础伤害是最高的。

虽然基础攻速最慢，但是我们很容易就能把攻速堆到7F，这也是所有弓的攻速上限。

在有9级狂热光环时，只需要达到面板105%攻速。

[5] Why Min/Max instead of ED? 为什么升级最大最小伤害而不是ED?

For corruption, 120%ED = 51.6 average damge, but 50-100 Damage = 75 average damage.

For upgrading, 50%ED = 21.5 average damge, but 15-30 Damage = 22.5 average damage.

One might say that we lost many AR.

Actually, we do not need to many AR, because Inner Sight reduces lots of enemy's defence.

We can easily get 95% hit rate after casting Inner Sight.

对于损坏和升级属性，ED都不如最大最小加伤害多，参看上面。

相比升Min/Max，升ED同时会附带AR，这部分AR其实不重要。

内视几乎可以把普通怪物的防御值降到0，这样几乎对所有小怪都能达到95%命中率上限。

[6] Why not CTA? 为什么不首选CTA?

Besides BO and BC, Warrior Untamed give us +1 Shout OSkill which increases character's defence.

Plus +1 Iron Skin on Ogre's Embrace(belt) and +15 Chilling Armor on Fortitude(Chest), Amazon will get 50K defence.

And mercenary will get 38-40K defence.

Warrior Untamed带有BO和BC的同时，它还带有大叫技能，可以增加队伍防御。

跟腰带上的铁布衫和衣服上的寒冰装甲叠加，人物可以得到5W防御值。

佣兵也能有接近4W防御值。
