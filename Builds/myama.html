Formulas 公式

Damage Formula 伤害计算公式

Dmg = { [ DmgB × ( 1 + EDW ) ] + DmgE } × ( 1 + EDN ) + EleDmg

DmgB = Base Damage. The damage value of weapon base item. 武器基础伤害（白字）

DmgE = Equipment Damage. Min/Max Damage value from any equipment attributes. 所有装备属性上的最大最小伤害值（蓝字）

EDW = ED from Weapon. Including that from jewelries embedded in weapon. 武器ED值，包含镶嵌在武器上的珠宝

EDN = ED from Non-Weapon. Including that from skills. 非武器ED值总和，包含技能加成

EleDmg = Element Damage. 元素伤害

Attack Speed Formula 攻速计算公式

AnimDuration = {(AnimLength * 256) / [AnimSpeed * (AnimRate + SIAS + EIAS - WSM) / 100]} -1
[EIAS = 120 * IASItem / (120 + IASItem)]

It's a little complex. Please see attack speed formula details.

We can lookup IAS breakpoints in D2planner or a simple calculator.

公式的具体解释参看这里。查询攻速断点可以用D2planner或者简易计算器。

Explanations 解释

[1] Why not Str or Vital points? 为什么不加力量和体力？

We will have enough Strength points from the crafted GC and belt, so we do not need extra Str.

We will have about 1.5K life after BO, so we do not need extra Vital.

超大符和腰带已经提供了足够的力量穿装备，所以不需要再加力量点。

使用BO以后可以达到1.5K血量，基本上死不了，所以不需要再加体力点。

[2] Why Guided Arrow but not Strafe? 为什么用导引箭而不用炮轰？

With D2RR, one shot of guided arrow can hit one single target 5 times at most just like 1.09.

It's much faster killing a boss using guided arrow than strafe with nearly 100% pierce.

现在可以像1.09版本一样，一发导引箭可以击中单个目标最多5次。

当穿刺概率接近100%，打BOSS用导引箭比炮轰要快得多。
