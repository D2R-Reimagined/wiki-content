IMPORTANT: Because most of the options below either adjust a single file or alter many files, when a new version of Reimagined gets installed you will have to reapply every tweak manually afterwards.  Keep this in mind when deciding which of these are of interest.  You could use all of them together if you wanted.

 

NOTE: These are advanced game changes, where extra steps are required beyond the basic game & mod installation.  It is recommended to first experience the mod by default before attempting these advanced changes.  Some of these were found by players & others by the mod developers, so support is not guaranteed if the steps are not followed.  Be ready & willing to reinstall the game before continuing.

 

Expanded Stash

This increases the shared stash tabs from X to X.  Every additional tab is able to hold 2.5M gold, so this increases the total amount of gold that can be held too.

 

Forced Terror Zones

This forces the following very popular zones to always be terrorized!  While changing your computer system's clock can extend throne of destruction or cow level sessions, keeping the popular areas terrorized makes things easier.

 

Removing Potions/Scrolls from most monster drops

With potions and scrolls mostly gone off the ground, missing important gear becomes less of an issue.

 

Remove the Splash Charm's dust graphic

When using the Splash Charm, a giant cloud of dust is present letting the gamer know how large the area for the effect is covering.  You can turn off this graphic, while the damage will continue to hit mobs making it easier to see things while fighting & is a little easier on your graphics card.

 

Expanded Aspect Ratio limit (19% zoomedout)

This increases the viewable area on-screen, where Blizzard has allowed upto 19:10 ratio to be used in the game.  This allows players to teleport horizontally much faster then a typical 16:9 ratio.  Currently the steps listed are for Nvidia videocard users only, works on 1080p & 4k monitors too.

 

Much Faster Loading game

This reduces the amount of time it takes for both the game's initial loading and also reduces the loading screens when changing inbetween acts/zones.  This has been reported to remove microstutters & slightly reduce the minimum system requirements (except for the hard drive storage size).
