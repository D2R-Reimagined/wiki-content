There has been a lot of rework in charms with this mod, from crafting gems to finding gems with various new/greater affixes on them. Even large charms can now be worthwhile! There are also new Unique charms to be hunted down!

We have also introduced a splash charm to give those melee builds an extra edge!

Splash Charm - Collin's Might

The Splash Charm (Collin's Lesser Might) is a new item obtained by completing the Den of Evil quest (killing Corpsefire as it's a guaranteed drop from him) on Normal difficulty. This charm is designed for melee builds, slightly lowering their output damage while granting a small area of effect (AoE) in which they deal damage. The level requirement increases with each tier and is equal to the rune used to make it.

Torch Upgrade System

 

Acquiring a Hellfire Torch

Each Prime Evil in Ubers will drop a Soul (Mephisto's Animus, Baal's Calamity, Diablo's Ire). Transmuting these three items in the Horadric Cube will produce a base level Hellfire Torch.

*Only one Hellfire Torch can be spawned in per game.

 

How to Upgrade

To upgrade your Hellfire Torch, you need to have: Standard of Heros + Orb of Corruption + Hellfire Torch.

 

Obsidian Beacon

The Obsidian Beacon is the final form of the Hellfire Torch.
