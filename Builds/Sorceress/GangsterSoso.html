Gangster Soso

Overview

A highly experimental hybrid Sorceress build that combines Enchant buffed Skeletons, Multishot Bow Attacks, and Energy Shield for survivability.

Gear Setup

Weapon: Beeswarm (Crusader Bow)

Base: Upgrade Long Battle Bow twice → Crusader Bow

Sockets: 6

Socketed: 6x Fire Jewels

Upgrades: 10x Fire Damage upgrades

Helm: Overlord's Helm

Upgrades: 3x Fire Skill upgrades

Socketed: 4x Fire Jewels

Armor: ShadowTrick

Upgrades: 5x All Res and Max Res upgrades

Socketed: 2x Fire Jewels, 2x All Skills Jewels

Don't use ‘Fade’, you must use ‘Bust of speed’

Gloves: Burning Soul

Upgrades: 3x Projectile Pierce (crucial for bow attacks)

Quiver: Quiver of Resistance and Slaughter

20% Chance to cast Level 5 Lower Resist on hit

+20% Attack Speed

+25% Pierce

Optional, but synergizes well with the build

Belt: Crafted Belt (Spiderweb Sash preferred)

Crafted with +1 All Skills and Elemental Damage affixes

Upgrades: 3x All Stats + Res

 

Amulet: Crypt King

 

Rings: 2x Stone of Jordan

 

Boots: Bone Mesh

 

Stat Allocation

Strength: Enough to wear gear (e.g., armor requirement is high)

Dexterity: Enough for gear

Vitality: Skip

Energy: Dump the rest (for Energy Shield tanking)

 

 Skill Tree

Fire

Enchant – Max

Warmth – Max

Fire Mastery – Max

Lightning

Energy Shield – 1 point

Telekinesis – Max

Rest of points into Energy Shield

Cold

Skip entirely

Mercenary Setup

Weapon: Infinity (Conviction Aura)

Helm: Battle Prowess (grants Fanaticism Aura)

Armor: Flexible (Merc rarely dies due to skeletons tanking)

Note:
Even with Fanaticism aura, you still need Burst of Speed to hit IAS breakpoints unless you socket 4x IAS jewels. Shadow Trick remains essential.

Breakpoints & Mechanics

IAS: Must reach 102 IAS if relying only on Fanaticism – impractical without Burst of Speed

FCR: 63% FCR is sufficient for teleport

Pierce: Gloves + Quiver gives enough

Fire Pierce: Easily capped with jewels & gear (over -115)

Gameplay Notes

You absolutely have to skip bosses. Are you listening? If you try to kill bosses in an 8-player game, you're going to regret it.

Final Thoughts

This build brings to life a classic fantasy – Enchanting your own minions instead of relying on a Necro friend. While not the most meta or efficient build, it offers a fresh and creative gameplay experience in D2R Reimagined. Perfect for players who want to try something different and have fun doing it.
