The Chosen Wolf and his Seven Sisters

Baft's Shadow Master TrapSin Build

Introduction:

Shadow Master has a few hidden functions that are pretty neat,
The ability to use any spell in the Assassin's arsenal scaling with points invested,
Summoned with ilvl 99 gear for every slot once  you're at 17 hard points ,
 Combine this with a tonne of auras along with enchants from your chosen wolf merc
Then watch your band of cutthroats clear the screen whilst a valkyrie looks over them.
 

Skills✔
Traps:

25 Shock Web (Synergy)
25 Charged Bolt Sentry (Synergy)
25 Lightning Sentry (Main Ability we spam)

Shadow Disciplines:

1 Claw Mastery (Prerequisite)
1 Burst of Speed (Prerequisite)
1 Weapon Block (1 point Wonder for all the defence it packs)
1 Fade (Main Buff)
1 Shadow Warrior (Prerequisite)
25 Shadow Master (Your unkillable meat shields)

Now assuming you're level 100 with all your skill points you will have 6 spare skill points
It's ultimately upto you here, you can put them in Weapon Block or Fade for more defence,
However Shadow Masters are more likely to use an ability you have skill points in, with that said,
You can drop 1 point into multiple untouched skills here to give the RNG in their rotation a boost.

Rotation:

Equip Salvation Aura (Resistance and Elemental Immunity for the Shadow Masters)
Weapon Switch,
Cast Battle Command, (Skill point)
Cast Shadow Master 3 Times
Cast Fade (Resistances)
Cast Oak Sage, (Health)
Cast Battle Orders, (Health & Mana)
Cast Shout, (Defence)
Weapon Switch,
Cast Shadow Master 2 Times, (Our extra 2 from Weapons)
Cast Valkyrie, (The 7th Sister)
Find Enemies
Cast Lightning Sentry, Until Max Traps
Spam Shock Web until you need more Lightning Sentries.

Then when you come across a pack you can easily drop you can drop the buffs and do the following:

Cast Shadow Master 5 Times,
Cast Valkyrie, (The 7th Sister)
Cast Fade, (Resistances)
Cast Oak Sage, (Health)
Find Enemies
Cast Lightning Sentry, Until Max Traps
Spam Shock Web until you need more Lightning Sentries.

 

 

GEAR✔
(The Juicy Part!) Do yourself a favour in the long run and don't chase CORRUPTIONS I'll put them here for good measure regardless.

Weapons:
Twin Sin Sisters: With these being Runic Talons we don't need a lot of Attack speed to reach our trap placement breakpoint,
The main reason we take these is for the extra Shadow Masters and for our black sheep and 7th Sister of the family the Valkyrie.

Enchants(5): +10% Light Skill Damage/-10% Enemy Light Res
Sockets(3): 3 Spring Facet Jewels >> 3 Heaven Facet Jewels
Enchants(5): +6% Light Skill Damage/-6% Enemy Light Res 30% Faster Cast Rate (For smooth Teleporting)
Sockets(3): 1 Guardian's Thunder Jewel/2 Spring Facet Jewels >> 3 Heaven Facet Jewels
CORRUPTIONS: 1 to all skills >> Elemental Damage/Enemy Resistances (There is Faster Cast Rate combos of these 2 stats if you can't get the main one)

 

Helm:
Crafted Elemental Diadem: You're looking at a minimum of 2 to All skills and 1 to  Assassin skills ( it can roll with +2) Use a Griffon's Eye until this point.
These crafts can roll 1-3 prefixes and suffixes so you can get some amazing outcomes if you're patient and farm enough, Some ideal Affixes would be:
Life, +Discpline or Trap Skills, %Lightning Damage, - Enemy Lightning Res, Faster Hit Recovery and Cast Static 9/Energy Shield 13 when struck
(If you roll a 3 All Skills 2 Assassin Skills 3 Traps/Shadow Disciplines with either of those when strucks, I'll eat my shoes).

Enchants(3): +6 to Lightning skills >> +3 All Skills
Sockets(4): 4 Spring Facet Jewels >> Heaven Facet Jewels
CORRUPTIONS: 1 to all skills >> Max Res >> Physical Damage Reduction >> Max Life/Mana

 

Chestplate:
Shadowtrick: More traps, more skills , more damage and a free Conviction aura to boot, equivilent to -60 Enemy Resistances.

Enchants(5): +25 All Resistances/+5 Max All Resistances >> 40% Physical Damage Reduction
Sockets(4): 4 Spring Facet Jewels >> Heaven Facet Jewels
CORRUPTIONS: 1 to all skills >> Max Res >> Physical Damage Reduction >> Max Life/Mana

 

Gloves:
Rapturous Blessings(Upgraded: These bad boys will make our Shadow Masters immune to the elements, Sorting out our own resistances all through the use of the Salvation aura.

Enchants(3): 7% Increased Attack speed(Optional Breakpoint) 12% Max Health
CORRUPTIONS: 1 to Iron Skin(Huge Defence) >> Elemental Damage >> Block Chance

 

Belt:
The Saboteur's Bandolier: The go to for Traps in my humble ineducated opinion, the Attack speed helps us towards the optional 86% Break point,
The tricky part on this piece is getting the random assassin skill to roll Shadow Masters or Lightning Sentry

Enchants(3): 150 Life/Mana/Enhanced Defence
CORRUPTIONS: 1 to all skills >> Faster Hit Recovery//Faster Run/Walk

 

Boots:
Shadow Dancer: The Piece that inspired the build, Make sure you have +2 to Shadow Masters, Faster running and Hit Recovery is always nice too.

Enchants(3): Faster Hit Recovery if you need it, Otherwise 24% Health/Mana
CORRUPTIONS: 1 to all skills >> Max Res >> Physical Damage Reduction >> Max Life/Health Regen

 

Amulet:
Sigil Of Hope: 70-120% HP Scaling damage on Corpse Explosion, 2 to all skills and Resistance made this one a no-brainer.

Enchants(2): +2 All Skills 
CORRUPTIONS: 1 to all skills >> Max Res >> Physical Damage Reduction >> Max Life/Life Per Kill

 

Ring 1:
Jordan's Sigil: Stone of Jordan eat your heart out mate, there's a bigger better ring in town.

Enchants(2): 8% Physical Damage Reduction
CORRUPTIONS: 1 to all skills >> Max Res >> Physical Damage Reduction >> Max Life/Mana

 

Ring 2:
Decision Paralysis: There is so many rings you can opt into here 2nd Sigil of Jordan, Faerie Ring, Fellowship's Hope, Wisp Projector even, but I'm opting into Nature's Peace
the Oak Sage on this ring is a % scaling Health Buff!, The downside? He's made of paper… Regardless of your choice here, the enchants are the same. You can cheese this ring,
equipping It casting Oak Sage then equipping your Second Choice back on, the Oak Sage stays up despite the ring not being equipped, I tend to avoid this, it feels unintentional.

Enchants(2): 8% Physical Damage Reduction
CORRUPTIONS: 1 to all skills >> Max Res >> Physical Damage Reduction >> Max Life/Mana
