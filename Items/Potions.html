New Potions

Magic Find, Experience, and Sunder potions have been added and have a 30 second duration. They currently can only be purchased at a vendor. The potions will not “stack” as it grants a temporary state bonus.

Sunder

Sunder potions will break immunities similar to a sunder charm. The effects last 30 seconds and you can have multiple potions active. Unlike the sunder potions there are no negative effects that these potions give.
