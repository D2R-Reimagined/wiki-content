Build Overview

The Venom Whirlwind Assassin is a unique hybrid build that combines the poison damage of Venom with the physical AoE of Whirlwind, granted through Whirling Dervish boots. This build avoids using Chaos claws(budget), instead focusing on high-damage claws and trap synergy for crowd control. It excels in clearing dense packs in PvE, with Death Sentry traps handling mobs and Venom poisoning enemies during Whirlwind spins. Ideal for farming areas like Chaos Sanctuary and Travincal, it offers a fast-paced, mobile playstyle.

Buff character with CTA off hand, Venom, Burst of Speed, Anything else you have. Throw down Death sentrys in middle of monsters. Use Whirlwind and blast them away. Rinse repeat.

Pros

  • High poison and physical AoE damage
  • Quickly clear packs with corpse explosion provided by Death Sentry traps
  • Fun and mobile Whirlwind playstyle that will shred Nightmare TZ or Hell p1
  •  

Cons

  • Relies on boots with Whirlwind
  • Can be tricky to make tanky
  • High gear investment for optimal performance / Completing Ubers.

Skill Allocation

This build focuses on the Shadow Disciplines tree for Venom and utility, with points in Traps for clear speed, Whirlwind is granted by boots.

Stat Allocation

Prioritize enough Strength and Dexterity for gear, with the rest into Vitality for survivability. Energy is unnecessary with mana leech.

  • Strength: 95-120
  • Dexterity: 75-100
  • Vitality: Rest (maximize for HP)
  • Energy: Base (mana leech covers needs)

Endgame Strategy

Focus on farming high-density areas with Whirlwind and Venom, using Death Sentry for clearing mobs. A Might mercenary with good gear boosts physical damage.

  • Farming Areas: Chaos Sanctuary, Travincal, Pits, Ancient Tunnels, Entire Acts
  • Mercenary: Act 2 Might with Insight polearm, Treachery armor, As many +skills as possible
  • Breakpoints: Aim for 80% IAS (Insane WW Poison Procs), 48% FHR for survivability / mobility
