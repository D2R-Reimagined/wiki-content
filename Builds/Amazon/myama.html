Formulas 公式

Damage Formula 伤害计算公式

Dmg = [ DmgB × ( 1 + EDW ) + DmgE ] × ( 1 + EDN + Dex ÷ 100 ) + EleDmg

DmgB = Base Damage. The damage value of weapon base item. 武器基础伤害（白字）

DmgE = Equipment Damage. Min/Max Damage value from any equipment attributes. 所有装备属性上的最大最小伤害值（蓝字）

EDW = ED from Weapon. Including that from jewels embedded in weapon. 武器ED值，包含镶嵌在武器上的珠宝

EDN = ED from Non-Weapon. Including that from skills. 非武器ED值总和，包含技能加成

Dex = Total Dexterity points. 总敏捷点数

EleDmg = Element Damage. 元素伤害

Attack Speed Formula 攻速计算公式

AnimDuration = {(AnimLength * 256) / [AnimSpeed * (AnimRate + SIAS + EIAS - WSM) / 100]} -1
[EIAS = 120 * IASItem / (120 + IASItem)]

It's a little complex. Please see attack speed formula details.

We can lookup IAS breakpoints in D2planner or a simple calculator.

公式的具体解释参看这里。查询攻速断点可以用D2planner或者简易计算器。

HP Formula 血量计算公式

HP = [ HPB + HPCL × ( Lvl - 1 ) + Vit × HPV + HPE ] × ( 1 + HP% ) + HPEL × Lvl 

Lvl = Character Level. 角色等级

Vit = Total Vitality Points, including that from equipment. 活力点数，包含装备属性

HPB = Character Base HP. (AMA=50, Mizan=74) 角色基础HP（1级时血量）（亚马逊为50，米山为74）

HPCL = Character Increased HP per Level. (AMA=2, Mizan=10) 角色每级增加的HP（亚马逊为2，米山为10）

HPV = Increased HP per Vitality point. (AMA=3, Mizan=0) 每1点活力增加的HP（亚马逊为3，米山为0）

HPE = Fixed HP from Equipment, not HP per Level. 装备上增加的确定的HP值，非每级增加HP

HPEL = HP per Level from Equipment. 装备上每级增加的HP

HP% = Increase Max HP percentage from Equipment, including skill bonus(like BO). 装备上的增加血量上限，包含技能加成（比如BO）

Critical Striking and Piercing Formula 暴击和穿刺计算公式

P = 1 - ( 1 - PP ) × ( 1 - PS )

P = Final Probability of Double Damage or Piercing. 暴击或穿刺的最终几率

PP = Probability of Deadly Strike or Piercing Attack on Stat Panel. 属性面板上的致死打击或穿刺几率

PS = Probability of Critical Strike or Pierce from skills. 技能面板上的致命攻击或穿刺几率

Piercing Attack Hits Formula 穿刺攻击命中次数公式

For Guided Arrow:

E(H) = 5 - (1 - P) × (P4 + 2 × P3 + 3 × P2 + 4 × P)

E(H) = Avarage hit count for a single guided arrow. 单支导引箭命中怪物次数的期望值

P = Probability of Piercing. 穿刺几率

For other skills:

E(H) = 1 ÷ ( 1 - P )

E(H) = Avarage hit count if plenty of monsters. 当有足够多怪物时，理论上单支箭能穿透击中怪物个数的期望值

P = Probability of Piercing. 穿刺几率

Explanations 解释

[1] Why not Str or Vit points? 为什么不加力量和活力？

We will have enough Strength points from the crafted GC and belt, so we do not need extra Str.

We will have about 2K life after BO, so we do not need extra Vit.

超大符和腰带已经提供了足够的力量穿装备，所以不需要再加力量点。

使用BO以后可以达到2K血量，基本上死不了，所以不需要再加活力点。

[2] Why Guided Arrow but not Strafe? 为什么用导引箭而不用炮轰？

With D2RR, one shot of guided arrow can hit one single target 5 times at most just like 1.09.

It's much faster killing a boss using guided arrow than strafe with high PA(>95%).

现在可以像1.09版本一样，一发导引箭可以击中单个目标最多5次。

穿刺几率很高(>95%)，打BOSS用导引箭比炮轰要快得多。

[3] Why +25 Pierce? 为什么要加满穿刺？

Pierce is the best way for high PA.

High PA is the key point for kill bosses with Guided Arrow.

And in some scenarios with plenty of monsters, such as cow level, Multiple Shot also gets benefits from high PA.

25 hard points and about 15 soft points of Pierce, plus 25% PA from the quiver, can provide 96% PA in total.

Average damage of Guided Arrow with 25-level Pierce is about 22% higher that with 1-level Pierce.

获得高穿透几率最好的方法就是加穿刺被动技能。

高穿透是用导引箭快速击杀BOSS的关键点。

而且在有足够多怪物的场景，像牛关，多重箭也能从高穿透获得很高的收益。

穿刺技能25硬点加15软点，再加上箭袋25%PA，最终可以获得96%的穿刺几率。

穿刺加满时比只加1点穿刺，导引箭的平均伤害能高22%。

[4] Why Quicksilver? 为什么选择Quicksilver作为武器？

It is the best weapon, bar none.

Grand Matron Bow has the highest base damage than any other bows.

Although it is the slowest bow, we can easily archieve 7F attack speed, which is the highest speed, just like other bows.

With Level 9 Fanaticism Aura, we just need to get 105%IAS.

这是最好的武器，没有之一。

大院长弓的基础伤害是最高的。

虽然基础攻速最慢，但是我们很容易就能把攻速堆到7F，这也是所有弓的攻速上限。

在有9级狂热光环时，只需要达到面板105%攻速。

[5] Why Min/Max instead of ED? 为什么升级最大最小伤害而不是ED?

For corruption, 120%ED = 51.6 average damge, but 50-100 Damage = 75 average damage.

For upgrading, 50%ED = 21.5 average damge, but 15-30 Damage = 22.5 average damage.

One might say that we lost many AR.

Actually, we do not need to many AR, because Inner Sight reduces lots of enemy's defence.

We can easily get 95% hit rate after casting Inner Sight.

对于损坏和升级属性，ED都不如最大最小加伤害多，参看上面。

相比升Min/Max，升ED同时会附带AR，这部分AR其实不重要。

内视几乎可以把普通怪物的防御值降到0，这样几乎对所有小怪都能达到95%命中率上限。

[6] Why not CTA? 为什么不首选CTA?

Besides BO and BC, Warrior Untamed give us +1 Shout OSkill which increases character's defence.

Plus +1 Iron Skin on Ogre's Embrace(belt) and +15 Chilling Armor on Fortitude(Chest), Amazon will get 50K defence.

And mercenary will get 38-40K defence.

Warrior Untamed带有BO和BC的同时，它还带有大叫技能，可以增加队伍防御。

跟腰带上的铁布衫和衣服上的寒冰装甲叠加，人物可以得到5W防御值。

佣兵也能有接近4W防御值。

[7] Why FCR and Res for secondary weapon suit? 为什么副武器套升级FCR和抗性?

When looting act bosses, we have to travel a long way.

But this build for MF(without Fortitude) has only 20%FCR from amulet.

In this case, we can TP much faster with the secondary weapon suit with high FCR.

On the way, Res can also keep us safe.

这套MF配装没有刚毅，所以FCR很低，只有项链上的20%。

当刷每幕BOSS的时候需要跑的路都比较远，切换到高FCR的副武器套就能更快的赶路。

而且副盾牌上的抗性还可以提升路上的安全。

[8] Why not Amplified quiver? 为什么不用带有伤害加深的箭袋?

Life Tap curse is the key point to keep mercenary alive, because Life Tap takes effect on Uber Meph and Uber Diablo but LF does not.

But Amplify curse will override Life Tap curse from mercenary, so do not use Amplified quiver for Uber Bosses.

For MF, the Amplified quiver is recommended.

装备上的偷取生命对超级劳模和超级大菠萝无效，但偷取生命诅咒有效，所以偷取生命诅咒能让佣兵不倒。

但是伤害加深诅咒会覆盖偷取生命诅咒，所以不要用带伤害加深的箭袋去打超级BOSS。

对于MF，最好还是用伤害加深箭袋。

[9] Why so much Res and DR? 为什么需要这么多抗性和物免?

Res is for Uber Meph, and DR is for all Uber Bosses and cow level.

Uber Mephisto has Level 20 Conviction Aura which decreases our all resistance by 125%.

When we fighting Uber Meph, we must have enough LR/CR to resist his cold and light skills.

To reach the high resistance limit(Ama 85%, Merc 75%) in Hell difficulty, we should have 330% LR/CR on stat panel for Ama and 320% for Merc.

Also, all Uber Bosses have physical attacks, so enough DR is needed.

The high limit of DR is 50%, which is easy to reach.

高抗性是为了对付超级劳模，高物免是为了所有超级BOSS和牛关。

超级劳模自带20级信念灵气，会降低125%的全抗。

因为超级劳模的技能有冰系和电系两种，所以必须堆足够的电抗和冰抗。

想要在地狱难度达到抗性上限（主角85 佣兵75），必须把主角的面板上电抗和冰抗堆到330%，佣兵320%。

超级BOSS的物理伤害也很高，所以物免也要堆高。

物免的上限是50%，这个还是很容易能达到的。

[10] Why Heaven's Treasure for MF? 为什么MF用Heaven's Treasure?

Upgrading Heaven's Treasure by 30%MF 2-4 times, which makes its MF reach the upper limit 411%, is the easiest way to increase Ama's MF.

Comparing to BIS for Uber Bosses, only 1 equipment is replaced for MF.

400%MF is enough, so I do not recommend Constricting Ring any more, which will lower Ama's damage.

Heaven's Treasure升级2-4次30%MF就能达到411%的上限，这是提升MF值最简单的方法。

与打超级BOSS的毕业装相比，MF装只换了1件装备。

一般来说，400%MF值已经足够了，所以没必要再装备收缩戒指了。

[11] Why not Act 2 Merc? 为什么不用米山?

Actually, Mizan with Might Aura and Pride weapon can provide the most ED for Ama.

The root reason is Mizan is too weak to keep alive against Uber Bosses.

  1. Act 5 Dual-Wield Merc has Iron Skin passive skill which increase Merc's defence a lot.
  2. Act 5 Dual-Wield Merc has Taunt skill which decrease enemies' physical damage and hit rate a lot.
  3. Act 5 Dual-Wield Merc has Frenzy skill which increase Merc's attack speed a lot resulting in more frequent leeching life.
  4. Act 5 Dual-Wield Merc can equip Last Wish with Fade skill which provide 56%Res and 11%DR and reduce curse duration by 75%.
  5. Act 5 Dual-Wield Merc can equip Last Wish with Life Tap curse which make Merc able to leech life from Uber Meph/Diablo.

Another reason is Beast has Fanaticism Aura that provides a lot of IAS.

In this way, Ama only needs 105%IAS to reach 7F attack speed, otherwise 292%IAS is needed.

实际上，用力量灵气的米山带骄傲，能最大程度提升主角的伤害。

不用米山的最根本原因就是他在对付超级BOSS的时候很难站得住。

  1. 双持蛮子佣兵的铁布衫被动可以大幅提升佣兵防御。
  2. 双持蛮子佣兵的嘲弄技能可以大幅降低怪物的物理伤害和命中率。
  3. 双持蛮子佣兵的狂乱技能可以大幅提升攻击速度，这样可以更频繁触发吸血。
  4. 双持蛮子佣兵的武器最后希望带有能量消解技能，可以提升56%全抗和11%物免，还能减少75%被诅咒时间。
  5. 双持蛮子佣兵的武器最后希望带有偷取生命诅咒，可以让超级劳模和超级大菠萝变成可吸血的状态。

还有一个原因，就是武器野兽带有狂热光环，能提供大量的攻速。

在狂热光环的加持下，主角只需要105%IAS就能达到7F攻速，否则需要292%IAS才能达到。

[12] Why not Royal Diadem? 为什么不首选Royal Diadem?

Use Royal Diadem only if Ama and Merc's LR/CR does not reach 330/320.

When using Royal Diadem, keep closer to Merc due to the small range of Level 1 Salvation Arua.

只有当主角和佣兵的冰抗和电抗无法达到330/320的时候才用Royal Diadem。

1级救助灵气的范围很小，所以当用Royal Diadem的时候，主角要离佣兵近一些。
