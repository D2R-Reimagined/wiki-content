General Changes

 

Skill Trees

Shadow Discipline Skills

Shadow Warrior

  • Mana cost reduced

Shadow Master

  • Mana cost reduced

Martial Arts Skills 

  • Charge duration increased to 30s from 15s
  • Charge duration shows in tooltip

Fist of Fire

  • No longer converts physical damage to fire damage for consistency with other elemental attacks

Trap Skills

Blade Sentinel

  • Now does 100% of weapon damage from 75%
  • Damage bonuses doubles at higher skill levels to keep it competitive in higher difficulties.
  • Blade Sentinel can now fly so it won't have to path around terrain
  • No longer has a casting delay
  • Has a scaling mana curve
  • Move speed increased by 50%
  • Can now hit every 15 frames (was 25)
  • Now gains AR and AR per lvel

Blade Fury

  • Has been reworked and is no longer a channeled attack
  • Now throws a shuriken that bounces to up to 2 nearby enemies (2 bounce limit doesn't scale)
  • Shuriken throw rate is based on attack speed
  • Gains 1 projectile per 8 soft skill levels. (12 total at skill level 100)
  • Initial throw projectiles can pierce enemies
  • Carries weapon damage and modifiers

Blade Shield

  • Damage curves increased at higher skill levels
  • Mana cost reduced
  • Delay between hits reduced
  • Can now hit every 15 frames (was 25)
  • Now gains AR and AR per level
