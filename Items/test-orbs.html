Orb of Conversion

An Orb of Conversion is a very rare item that can turn a Rare item into an equivalent Unique item.  

This provides players with a chance to obtain powerful items without having to find them the traditional way.

An Orb of Conversion can drop from any monster in the game, on any difficulty.

To use an Orb of Conversion, transmute it with a Rare item in the Horadric Cube.

Orb of Assemblage

The Orb of Assemblage is a very rare allows players to turn Rare items into an equivalent Set item.  

This provides players with a chance to complete sets much easier without having to find them all the traditional way.

An Orb of Assemblage can drop from any monster in the game, on any difficulty.

To use an Orb of Assemblage, transmute it with a Rare item in the Horadric Cube.

Orb of Infusion

The Orb of Infusion is a relatively common item allows players to turn White base items (or Magic Jewelry) into an equivalent Rare item.

An Orb of Infusion can drop from any monster in the game, on any difficulty.

To use an Orb of Infusion, transmute it with a white base item (or Magic Jewelry) in the Horadric Cube.

Orb of Shadows

The Orb of Shadows is an extremely rare item allows players to force items to be Ethereal.

The item must have durability, therefore Bows, Crossbows and certain swords cannot be made ethereal.

An Orb of Shadows only drops from Unique and Super Unique monsters in Hell Terror Zones

To use an Orb of Shadows, transmute it with an item in the Horadric Cube.

Orb of Socketing

The Orb of Socketing is a useful tool that allows players to add a random number of sockets to an item.

The Orb of Socketing can be used to reroll sockets on items that already have them.

Each item has a max number of sockets, depending on its level.

Simply transmute an Orb of Socketing with an item in the Horadric Cube.

The Orb of Socketing replaces essence drops from Act bosses and Key Wardens in Nightmare and Hell. They can also drop from Champion, Unique and Super Unique monsters in Nightmare and Hell Terror Zones.

Orb of Corruption

The Orb of Corruption is a risk/reward item that allows players to potentially add an additional affix to an item. However, there are non-beneficial outcomes as well, so use wisely!

Transmute an Orb of Corruption with a weapon, armor, or shield in the Horadric Cube. You must press the transmute button twice to properly apply the corruption.

Once an item has been corrupted you cannot corrupt it again.

See below for a list of possible affixes and their outcome chance.

NOTE!

DO NOT CORRUPT RUNEWORDS - Corrupt the base item first, and then craft your Runeword.

When corrupting SET items you must remove all other items from that set from your character first.

Put related set items in shared stash or drop them on ground.

Failing to do so will apply the corruption to the set bonus and will only be effective when set bonuses are active.
