This will be an in-depth guide to end game amazon physical bow. You will conquer every single piece of content with this build in 2.0.5. This is an expensive build and takes a lot of rerolling to achieve correct results. Wish you the best of luck on corruptions.

Designed for ranged dominance, leveraging Multishot as your primary attack while using Inner Sight to debuff enemies’ defense, making them easier to one shot. Let's break it down:

Build Overview

  • Primary Skill: Multishot / Magic Arrow. (Multi 80% of the time. Magic for areas higher immune to physical)
  • Bossing Skill: Guided Arrow (Pierce, Multi Hit, CB)
  • Support Skill: Inner Sight (Reduces enemy defense)
  • Playstyle: Ranged, fast-paced, area-clearing bowazon
  • Stat Priority: All dexterity, Strength and vitality will come from gear / charms
  • Difficulty: intermediate; requires good gear for endgame

Skill Point Allocation

Focus on the Bow and Crossbow tree for damage and the Passive and Magic tree for utility. Here’s the recommended progression:

Core Skills

  1. Multishot (25 points)
    • Your main attack. Fires multiple arrows in a wide arc, shredding groups of enemies. Max this for maximum damage output.
  2. Inner Sight (25 points)
    • Lowers enemy defense significantly at higher levels, boosting your hit chance and that of your party. Max this for the build’s theme and utility.
  3. Guided Arrow (25 points)
    • Boss killing machine. Use when you have a high defense and HP monster or boss. Skill will hit multiple times and apply all debuffs.
  4. Magic Arrow (1 point)
    • Better for clearing on different acts vers multi shot.
  5. Critical Strike (5-10 points)
    • Increases your chance to deal double damage. A few points here go a long way due to diminishing returns. Having deadly strike on gear will synergize really well)
  6. Pierce (5-10 points)
    • Allows your arrows to pass through enemies, hitting multiple targets per shot. Synergizes beautifully with Multishot. Aim for 50-70% pierce chance with gear. (Quiver will give you +25% Pierce so aim for 75% in tree. Do not use any other gear for pierce)
  7. Penetrate (5-10 points)
    • Boosts your Attack Rating, ensuring Multishot hits consistently.

Optional/Support Skills

  • Dodge/Avoid/Evade (1 point each)
    • These passives reduce damage taken from melee, ranged, and while moving. One point each is enough with +skills.

Skill Point Plan

  • By level 80-90, aim for:
    • 25 Multishot
    • 25 Inner Sight
    • 5 Guided arrow
    • 10 Critical Strike
    • 1 Pierce
    • 10 Penetrate (important to keep AR up)
    • 1 Dodge, Avoid, Evade
    • Remaining points: situational skills depending on gear.

Stat Point Allocation

  • Strength: All strength will come from crafted grand charms.
  • Dexterity: Primary stat. Pump this for damage and Attack Rating.
  • Vitality: vitality will come from crafted grand charms. (Change gear rolls for vitality if needed)
  • Energy: Zero points.

Gameplay Tips

  1. Positioning: teleport in to room, Cast inner sight, Fire away with multi shot, Profit.
  2. HEALING:  switch to strafe one shot should be 100% HP
  3. Inner Sight Usage: Cast it frequently on enemy packs to lower their defense, especially against bosses or high-defense mobs. #######NCREASE ONSHOT SWAG 9999%#######
  4. Mana Management: Rely on mana leech from gear. Add ONE mana on kill if needed.
  5. Physical Immunes: Craft physical sunder charm with flat damage / bow skills / all resistance
  6. Farming Areas: Act 5 halls 1-3 for exp and keys from Nihlathak.
