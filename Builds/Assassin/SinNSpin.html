Build Overview

The Venom Whirlwind Assassin is a unique hybrid build that combines the poison damage of Venom with the physical AoE of Whirlwind, granted through boots like Gore Rider or crafted boots with Whirlwind charges. This build avoids using Chaos claws, instead focusing on high-damage claws and trap synergy for crowd control. It excels in clearing dense packs in PvE, with Lightning Sentry traps handling single-target damage and Venom poisoning enemies during Whirlwind spins. Ideal for farming areas like Chaos Sanctuary and Travincal, it offers a fast-paced, mobile playstyle.

Pros

  • High poison and physical AoE damage
  • Versatile with traps for single-target support
  • Fun and mobile Whirlwind playstyle

Cons

  • Relies on rare boots with Whirlwind charges
  • Poison immunes require trap backup
  • High gear investment for optimal performance

Skill Allocation

This build focuses on the Shadow Disciplines tree for Venom and utility, with points in Traps for Lightning Sentry synergy. Whirlwind is granted by boots, so no Claw Mastery investment is needed.

Skill Level Notes
Venom 20 Main poison damage source, applied during Whirlwind
Lightning Sentry 20 Primary trap for single-target and immune enemies
Death Sentry 10-20 Corpse explosion for crowd clear, adjust based on points
Shadow Master 10 Tank and distraction, don’t over-invest
Burst of Speed 1 Mobility and attack speed
Fade 1 Resistances and damage reduction, use as needed
Prerequisites (Fire Blast, Charged Bolt Sentry, etc.) 1 each Needed for Lightning Sentry

Gear Recommendations

Gear is split into two setups: a budget-friendly option for newer players or those with limited wealth, and an endgame setup for maximum performance. Both prioritize poison damage, attack speed, and survivability, with boots providing Whirlwind. Chaos claws are avoided.

Stat Allocation

Prioritize enough Strength and Dexterity for gear, with the rest into Vitality for survivability. Energy is unnecessary with mana leech.

  • Strength: 95-120 (enough for Enigma or Bartuc’s)
  • Dexterity: 75-100 (enough for claws, adjust for block if using shield swap)
  • Vitality: Rest (maximize for HP)
  • Energy: Base (mana leech covers needs)

Leveling Guide

Start with traps to safely clear areas, transitioning to Venom and Whirlwind once you acquire suitable boots (around level 40+).

  • Levels 1-10: Use Fire Blast and normal attacks, clear Blood Moor and Den of Evil.
  • Levels 11-20: Invest in Charged Bolt Sentry, farm Tombs in Act 2.
  • Levels 21-30: Max Lightning Sentry, add Death Sentry, clear Act 3 temples.
  • Levels 31-40: Start Venom, use Burst of Speed, farm Baal runs in Normal.
  • Levels 41+: Equip Whirlwind boots, max Venom, farm Nightmare Chaos Sanctuary.

Endgame Strategy

Focus on farming high-density areas with Whirlwind and Venom, using Lightning Sentry for bosses or poison immunes. A Might mercenary with good gear boosts physical damage.

  • Farming Areas: Chaos Sanctuary, Travincal, Pits, Ancient Tunnels
  • Mercenary: Act 2 Might (Nightmare Offensive) with Insight polearm, Treachery armor, and Andariel’s Visage
  • Breakpoints: Aim for 40% IAS (check claw breakpoints), 48% FHR for survivability
