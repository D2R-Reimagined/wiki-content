Potions

Health and Mana potions have been upgraded to be percent based if your HP or Mana are high enough. Health regeneration speed has normalized to be equal to Mana regeneration speed.

New Potions

Magic Find, Experience and Sunder potions have been added and have a 30 second duration. 

Potions can be purchased from vendors in all acts.

Potion effects do not stack - drinking another potion of the same type just resets the duration.

Sunder

Sunder potions will break immunities similar to a sunder charm. The effects last 30 seconds and you can have multiple potions active. Unlike the sunder charms there are no negative effects that these potions give.
