Power Orbs

These are special items that allow various crafting and item manipulation oppurtunities in order to bridge the gap from the single player experience and no trading to the multiplayer experience with trading.

They each have designated drop locations and requirements which are listed under each orb and detailed on this page.

Magic Find does not affect orbs.

Orb of Conversion

An Orb of Conversion is a very rare item that allows players to turn a Rare item into an equivalent Unique item. 

This provides players with a chance to obtain powerful items without having to find them the traditional way.

An Orb of Conversion can drop from any monster in the game, on any difficulty.

An Orb of Conversion drops on the first kill of Diablo in every difficulty.

To use an Orb of Conversion, transmute it with a Rare item in the Horadric Cube.

Does not work on jewels.

Orb of Assemblage

The Orb of Assemblage is a very rare item that allows players to turn a Rare item into an equivalent Set item. 

This provides players with a chance to complete sets much easier without having to find them all the traditional way.

An Orb of Assemblage can drop from any monster in the game, on any difficulty.

An Orb of Assemblage drops from the first kill of Mephisto in every difficulty.

To use an Orb of Assemblage, transmute it with a Rare item in the Horadric Cube.

Be sure to check and make sure a set item exists on the base first. When one does not it defaults to rare/magic as a return and still consumes the orb.

Orb of Infusion

The Orb of Infusion is a relatively common item that allows players to turn a White base item (or Magic Jewelry) into an equivalent Rare item.

An Orb of Infusion can drop from any monster in the game, on any difficulty.

An Orb of Infusion drops from the first kill of Duriel in every difficulty.

To use an Orb of Infusion, transmute it with a White base item (or Magic Jewelry) in the Horadric Cube.

Does not work on jewels.

Orb of Shadows

The Orb of Shadows is an extremely rare item that allows players to force an item to be Ethereal. It changes no other stats.

The item must have durability, and cannot already be Indestructible.

Bows, Crossbows and certain swords cannot be made ethereal as they have no durability. 

An Orb of Shadows only drops from Unique and Super Unique monsters in Hell Terror Zones.

To use an Orb of Shadows, transmute it with an item in the Horadric Cube.

Orb of Socketing

The Orb of Socketing is a useful tool that allows players to add a random number of sockets to an item.

Each item has a max number of sockets, depending on its level.  

Elite Helms [E] of all bases can have a max of 4 sockets in Reimagined.

Check online sources before attempting to transmute for all other bases and socket level requirements

To use an Orb of Socketing, transmute it with an item in the Horadric Cube. You can reroll existing sockets this way.

Orbs replace essence drops from Act bosses and Key Wardens in Nightmare and Hell.

You can also find them from Champion, Unique and Super Unique monsters in Nightmare and Hell Terror Zones.

Orb of Corruption

The Orb of Corruption is a risk/reward item that allows players to potentially add an additional property to an item.

However, there are non-beneficial outcomes as well, so use wisely!

Transmute an Orb of Corruption with a weapon, armor, or shield in the Horadric Cube. 

You must press the transmute button twice to properly apply the corruption.

Once an item has been corrupted you cannot corrupt it again.

See below for a list of possible corruptions.


Where to find Orb of Corruption

An Orb of Corruption will always drop from the first Baal kill in any difficulty.

Corruption Affixes By Gear

There is a 55% chance a corruption is successful , adding a beneficial property to an item.

  • 1% chance best outcome (top row)
  • 5-7% chance for all other outcomes

There is a 45% chance a corruption fails . A failure can be broken down into three outcomes:

  • 25% chance no property is added
  • 10% chance the item is converted to a Rare Item
  • 10% chance the item is converted to a White Item (or Magic for Jewelry)

DO NOT CORRUPT RUNEWORDS - Corrupt the base item first, and then craft your Runeword.

When corrupting set items you must remove all other items from that set from your character first. 

Put related set items in shared stash. Save and Exit the game, join a new game, and then corrupt your intended set item. 

Failing to do so will apply the corruption to the set bonus and will only be effective when set bonuses are active.
