Item Upgrade System

All weapons, armor and jewelry can be upgraded with various additional stats a certain number of times.

2H Weapons such as Bows, Crossbows, Polearms, Spears and ‘Slam’ weapons (2h Maces, 2h Axes, 2h Swords, Staves, etc.) can be upgraded 10 times.

1H Weapons, Shields and Chest Armor can be upgraded 5 times each.

Helms, Gloves, Belts and Boots can be upgraded 3 times each.

Jewelry can be upgraded 2 times each.  A cheaper & weaker option of existing upgrades are listed without color below.

NOTE!

When upgrading SET items you must remove all other items from that set from your character first.

Put related set items in shared stash or drop them on the ground.

Failing to do so will apply the upgrade to the set bonus and will only be effective when set bonuses are active.
