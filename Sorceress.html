General Changes

Cold Skills

Ice Bolt

  • Now gains 1 additional projectile per 5 hard points to a maximum of 5
  • Damage increased across all skill levels slightly
  • Synergies increased from 15 to 20%

Ice Blast

  • Now gains 1 additional projectile per 5 hard points to a maximum of 5
  • Damage slightly increased across all skill levels
  • Synergies increased from 8 to 10%

Glacial Spike

  • Now gains 1 additional projectile per 8 hard points to a maximum of 3
  • Synergies increased from 5 to 11%
  • Damage curve slightly increased across all levels

Blizzard

  • Casting delay reduced to 1 second

Frozen Orb

  • Delay reduced to 0.6 seconds
  • Synergy increased from 3 to 5%
  • Damage curves increased by 20% past skill level 29

Frozen Armor

  • Now grants 1% physical damage reduction per hard point

Shiver Armor

  • Now grants 1% physical damage reduction per hard point

Chilling Armor

  • Now grants 1% physical damage reduction per hard point

Fire Skills

Fire Bolt

  • Now grants 1 additional projectile per 5 hard points up to a maximum of 5

Fire Ball

  • Now gains 1 additional projectile per 10 hard points up to a maximum of 3

Fire Wall

  • Casting delay reduced to 0.6 seconds

Meteor

  • Casting delay reduced to 1 second

Warmth

  • Gains 1 life after each kill per hard point

Hydra

  • Now shoots exploding projectiles
  • Synergies increased from 3 to 4%

Lightning Skills

Lightning

  • Min damage increased to lessen the frequency of whiff shots that do almost nothing

Chain Lightning

  • Min damage increased to lessen the frequency of whiff shots that do almost nothing

Thunder Storm

  • Strikes now cast a small Nova dealing damage based on Thunder Storm
