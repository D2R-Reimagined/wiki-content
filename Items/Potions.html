Potions

Health and Mana potions have been upgraded to be percent based if your HP or Mana are high enough. Health regeneration speed has normalized to be equal to Mana regeneration speed.

Health Potions

  • HP1 30 or 25% HP total whichever is higher.
  • HP2 60 or 25% HP total whichever is higher.
  • HP3 100 or 33% HP total whichever is higher.
  • HP4 180 or 33% HP total whichever is higher.
  • HP5 320 or 50% HP total whichever is higher.

Mana Potions

  • MP1 20 or 25% MP total whichever is higher.
  • MP2 40 or 25% MP total whichever is higher.
  • MP3 80 or 33% MP total whichever is higher.
  • MP4 150 or 33% MP total whichever is higher.
  • MP5 250 or 50% MP total whichever is higher.

New Potions

Magic Find, Experience, and Sunder potions have been added and have a 30 second duration. They currently can only be purchased at a vendor. The potions will not “stack” as it grants a temporary state bonus.

Sunder

Sunder potions will break immunities similar to a sunder charm. The effects last 30 seconds and you can have multiple potions active. Unlike the sunder charms there are no negative effects that these potions give.
