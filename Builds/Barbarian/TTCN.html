A level 6 Fanaticism mercenary enjoys the bonus of 3 Barbarian skills, which increases the skill level.

If there is a good base item with 3 skills, you can make this runeword. A base item with 3 War Cry and 3 Battle Cry can obtain 6 skills through the runeword.

The helm can also be chosen Overlord's Helm

 

Main and off-hand weapons:

In the early stage of game，the idea of dual Spirit runewords is to reach 105 FCR (Faster Cast Rate) is sufficient. In the mid-term, craft the Heart of the Oak runeword, which provides high resistance and +3 to all skills. For the late stage, optional options include:

  • Two unique Dacian Falx, which can be quickly obtained through transformation orbs; or craft 2 all skills weapons .that randomly roll +3 to Battle Cry. It is recommended to obtain them directly through orb transformation; tests with 4 orbs have all succeeded regarding the number of skills.
  • The unique Nightscape (a two-handed Colossus Sword) is also recommended, which can be enhanced up to 10 times and can be socketed 6 times.

Output method: For enemies with low physical resistance, directly get up close and use "War Cry". For enemies with high physical resistance, first use "Battle Cry" to reduce their physical resistance, then use "War Cry".

To ensure the mercenary's survival, equip them with various physical damage reduction items, elemental damage absorption gear, and items that grant freeze immunity (such as the Raven Frost ring), as well as life leech attributes. The only thing the mercenary needs to provide is Meditation for mana regeneration.
