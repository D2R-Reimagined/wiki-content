A5 Frenzy Merc

Early items:

until you have Azurewraths run double Lawbringer or full moon runewords on the a5 merc. the goal is to get a high sanctuary aura to lower magic resist of nearby enemies. 

Youth body armor runeword for weaken on strike will help a bit to until the Authority runeword

 

 

Endgame Gear:

***************************************************** DO NOT USE ANY ADDITIONAL ITEMS WITH CURSE CHANCE TO CAST *****************************************************

With these Items, you will have lvl27+ Sanctuary aura and max IAS to proc Weaken as often as possible. Sanctuary and Weaken are the only way to put -magic res on a monster.

***************************************************** DO NOT USE ANY ADDITIONAL ITEMS WITH CURSE CHANCE TO CAST *****************************************************

Weapon: Azurewrath Unique Phase Blade x2 → Look for lvl 13 Sanctuary Aura on each

Helm: Authority runeword → Chance to cast Weaken on Striking

Boots: Hollowed Ground Unique Boneweave Boots → +2-5 Sanctuary Aura

 

Other Gear:

Armor: Unique Boneweave Tarrasque Hide or Unique Ornate Plate Adamantine Plate

Gloves: Unique Bramble Mitts: Mephisto's Claw

Ring: Set ring Chiad's Halo
