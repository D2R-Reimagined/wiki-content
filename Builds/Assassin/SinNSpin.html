Build Overview

The Venom Whirlwind Assassin is a unique hybrid build that combines the poison damage of Venom with the physical AoE of Whirlwind, granted through boots Whirling Dervish or crafted boots with Whirlwind charges. This build avoids using Chaos claws(budget only), instead focusing on high-damage claws and trap synergy for crowd control. It excels in clearing dense packs in PvE, with Death Sentry traps handling crowd damage and Venom poisoning enemies during Whirlwind spins. Ideal for farming areas like Chaos Sanctuary and Travincal, it offers a fast-paced, mobile playstyle.

Pros

  • High poison and physical AoE damage
  • Corpse Explosion clearing with Death Sentry
  • Fun and mobile Whirlwind playstyle that will demolish TZ Nightmare/hell

Cons

  • Relies on boots with Whirlwind // Chaos Claw Runeword (WEAK)
  • Squishy with out correct item upgrades applied
  • High gear investment for ubers

Skill Allocation

This build focuses on the Shadow Disciplines tree for Venom and utility, with points in Traps for Death Sentry synergy. Whirlwind is granted by boots.

Skill Level Notes
Venom 25 Main poison damage source, applied during Whirlwind
Claw Mastery 5-25 More Attack Rating
Death Sentry 5 Corpse explosion for crowd clear, adjust based on points
Shadow Master 10 Tank and distraction, don’t over-invest
Burst of Speed 1 Mobility and attack speed
Fade 1 Resistances and damage reduction, use as needed
Prerequisites (Fire Blast, Charged Bolt Sentry, etc.) 1 each Needed for Lightning Sentry

Stat Allocation

Prioritize enough Strength and Dexterity for gear, with the rest into Vitality for survivability. Energy is unnecessary with mana leech.

  • Strength: 95-120
  • Dexterity: 75-100
  • Vitality: Rest (maximize for HP)
  • Energy: Base (mana leech covers needs)

Endgame Strategy

Focus on farming high-density areas with Whirlwind and Venom, using Death Sentry for clearing mobs. A Might mercenary with good gear boosts physical damage.

  • Farming Areas: Chaos Sanctuary, Travincal, Pits, Ancient Tunnels, Entire Acts
  • Mercenary: Act 2 Might with Insight polearm, Treachery armor, As many +skills as possible
  • Breakpoints: Aim for 80% IAS (Insane WW Poison Procs), 48% FHR for survivability / mobility
