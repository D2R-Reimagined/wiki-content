1. INTRO

Bone Necro has some very welcomed added utility and dimention on Reimagined when compared to retail D2R, with the main aspect of it being the Energy Synergy the bone spells have.

The best way currently top ut that to use is with the Maadi set. The set gives high amounts of stats and Bone Spear skill levels, and ties everything with Energy Shield oSkill (that while it wont get you nowhere close to being chonky as a Sorc with it, still provides more than enough of a survival buffer on a class that already has plenty meatshields protecting it).

I am currently seeing about making Teeth (and, consequently, also make other gear options more appealing to Bone Necros in the future patches), but that would be just another viable option to build the char. When/if that happens, I will probably also put together a gear profile for that.

Where we will focusing on making most of Bonespear and the Maadi set.

3. REASONS TO PLAY THIS BONE SPEAR NECRO

  1. Cool gear considerations and stat allocation juggling during leveling.
  2. Very interesting ‘glass cannon’ build-orientaded in its mentality, but that is actually very safe.
  3. Very active playstyle.

 

4. REASONS TO NOT PLAY THIS BONE SPEAR NECRO

  1. Not the fastest clean. Even with 3 Spears, the spread of your damage is not that good, so you wont be screen clearing.
  2. Very slow start. The juggling between sustain and damage can be demanding during leveling as well.

 

 

5. MAIN STATS

Finally some subversion on the decades old D2 stat logic. You still put as much Strength you need to equip your item. You will put nothing into Dexterity and Vitality and everything else on Energy.

This is for endgame, ofc. During leveling, put as much Vitality you feel you need to be safe and sustain your Blood Casting (more on it later), and then put all you can in Energy, but don’t go overboard unless you are fine with it.

 

6. ENDGAME SKILL BUILD

For leveling, start with Teeth until you feel it starts dragging to much to kill stuff, then pivot to Spear. Currently Teeth damage absolutely does not hold at all at endgame, but it is decent enough to clear all normal and then some in NM, but don’t let it drag too much. With Teeth your boss killing is also quite bad, so pivoting to Spear early is good to help on that aspect. Bone Spear does that job well for all the regular gameplay, with Bone Spirit only pulling substantially ahead of Spear on single-target damage when you start stacking ludicrous amounts of Energy Synergy (like 700+ points).

Therefore, the endgame build I use is the following, for lvl 100 with all extra skill points:

  • 1 Teeth (Pre req only on a build trying to maximize Maadi and Bonespear)
  • 1 Corpse Explosion (Pre-req. Still a too good spell like ever, I just avoid using it cuz its lame)
  • 25 Bone Spear (Your bread and butter trash killer. It will end with numbers that are analogous to other magic builds single target tools, but it pierces and you can have up to 3. It wont have good spread to screen clean, but you will delete bunched up or lined up packs super fast.)
  • 25 Bone Spirit (Your single target tool once its damage starts to pull ahead. By the end, its damage is around 25% to 30% higher than Bone Spear)
  • 1 Bone armor (Another defensive layer, but since it provides no synergies to the damage skills, no way top ut more than 1 point.)
  • 25 Bone Wall (Synergy for both you damage spells and Bone Armor, which is the reason we prio this along with Bone Prison instead of Teeth. These should be the last thing you point points into after the damage spells and your summons).
  • 25 Bone Prison (Synergy for both you damage spells and Bone Armor, which is the reason we prio this along with Bone Wall instead of Teeth. These should be the last thing you point points into after the damage spells and your summons)
  • 1 Weaken (You can put more at the start, but at the end, 1 point will get you to -100 skill cap)
  • 1 Clay Golem (Pre req and a good ‘meat’ shield)
  • 1 Iron Golem (Pre req, good meat shield and the carries of na aditional aura for you at the end)
  • 1 Blood Golem (your most importante golem. You need this one up at all times because it boosts your damage by A LOT. At the midgame until early post-hell gearing stages you might want to spend more points here to make it easier to keep them alive as they are very important)
  • 1 Golem Mastery (To improve your meatshields)
  • 1 Summon Resist (Same as above)
  • 1 Fire Golem (because at this point why not, another good meatshield).
  • 0-1 Revive (Will need 2 points into pre-reqs too – but no need for Skeleton Mastery as you get that from your boots. If you go with this, it will add up to 112 skill points, so you will need to cut points into some other stuff or get them via Large Charms. In theory, this should be the optimal thing to do, as 3 points would be totally worth for 5 extra meatshields. I don’t use it because I HATE this skill and can’t wait to see it be yoinked from the game one day, but like I said, this would be optimal. If not using it, just put the last couple points into Teeth).

 

7. GEAR SETUP 

You are, of course, locked into using Maadi’s full set with this build. The set consists of Wand and Necro Head combo, helm, chestpiece and gloves. This leaves you with only belt, boots, jewelry and alternate weapon slots to customize.

Therefore, most of the things exposed here will be regarding gear customizatio (enchants and corruptions) and not selection. Just use whatever generalistic good caster gear you get during leveling.

 

7.1 MAADI’S PARADOX

The set bonuses grant you the biggest chunk of the set skill values, and also with some defensive tools in the form of flat life and cannot be frozen and Energy Shield oSkill. It also gives you resists, but those are somewhat less impactfull since your Energy Shield applies before them whenever you take damage. They are still important to be there if/when your EShield breaks down and you take damage directly into your life.

 

7.1.1. GLOVE SLOT – MAADI’S SILENCE

The gloves give you some good PDR/MDR numbers, that work very well in tandem with Energy Shield (MDR in particular, since the PDR will most likely only come into play after Bone Armor).

The main bonuses of the piece are the damage buffs you get on the 2 and 3 piece sets.

For corruption, 10 all attributes is the optimal one here

Regarding item enhancements, for leveling you can get life and LPK enchants to help your sustain, for endgame though the optimal enchants are 3x 10 Vit and Energy enchants.

 

7.1.2. ARMOR SLOT – MAADI’S SPIRIT

The piece is a heavy stat-oriented piece, both on its main stats and set bonuses. Also gives some mana for more ‘virtual hp’ and MDR.

For corruption, the optimal one is 1 all skills, but the more attainable ones would be one of the defensive variants (indestructible and defense, or block chance/rating) or the life/mana variants (flat or 5% of each). Nothing of that much impact after 1 all skills.

For enchants, 5x 10 Vit and 15 Energy are the best ones for endgame.

 

7.1.3. HEAD SLOT – MAADI’S VISION

Generalist piece, both in the base stats and set. Provides some FHR, FRW, FCR, good chunk of Vitality and a few resists.

For corruption, the optimal one is 1 all skills, but this is another slot that can roll 10 all atributes, so that should be the more attainable goal.

For enchants, you can choose between 6 magic skills and 3 all skills. All skills may sound appealing at first glance as it also helps your golems and curse, but I would honestly just go for 6 magic skills all the time here as the extra 3 skill on the summons are barely felt at the end and you cap your curse effect anyways).

 

7.1.4. WEAPON SLOT – MAADI’S TORCH

Provides skills, 1 additional Bone Spear projectile and a WHOLE LOT of FCR. Only use the 2 all skills variants to try to corrupt, as that is the only roll that matters on the item so it can be aimed for quite easily.

For corruption, 1 all skill is the only one that matters here, but its not something I recommend farming for if you play fairly (I didnt and never will xD). The FCR corruptions are not that bad while you are in the processo f gearing as you might have sub-optimal rings and bad roll ranges on other pieces, but at the end you will overcap FCR most likely as Necro only needs 125, so they are not optimal too. The only other thing that gives some benefit is the MF/GF corrupt. Nothing else matters.

For enchants, 5x +2% magic damage -2% Enemy magic resist upgrades are a lock once you find the wand variance you are happy with.

 

7.1.5. SHIELD SLOT – MAADI’S SOUL

Another piece with skills, FCR and Spear Projectiles. It also has some good defensive bônus, that you wont put much to use with all your ‘boys’ running intercept for you.

For corruptions, once again, 1 all skills is optimal, but the more attainable ones would be one of the defensive variants (indestructible and defense, or block chance/rating) or the life/mana variants (flat or 5% of each). Nothing of that much impact after 1 all skills.

For enchants, 5x 10 Vit and 12 Energy are the best ones for endgame.
